The Story

We mine luxury.
We don't live it.

Opal mining in Andamooka hasn't changed much in decades. Small Gougers. Hard ground.

You read the dirt. You follow what the earth gives you. Some days it gives you nothing — but you dig anyway.

And then you see that flash of colour. And then it's gone… into someone else's ring, someone else's collection, someone else's life. Most people never think about the hands it passed through to get there.

Once a stone leaves the claim, where it came from is forgotten. It becomes part of the trade. That's always been the deal.

We want to change that — and we know how.

"And then you see that flash of colour. And then it's gone."

Watch for colour after rain. Good opal follows the old water. It always has.

Who We Are

Matt & Cozza Kathagen

Brothers. Miners. They've been digging deep their whole lives.

You might know them from Outback Opal Hunters, but in outback South Australia that's not what they're known for.

They're known for showing up — to the claim, to the community, to the work.

To them, this was never a game.

It was never about money.

What started as an adventure became their livelihoods.

While there's no glamour at the bottom of a mine shaft, or comfort in the dirt, you better believe there's still adventure.

We've given up a lot to keep doing this. Comfort, certainty, the kind of life most people take for granted.

We've been below sea level so long, all we see is level.

It's simple. We go underground to keep our family above it.

What we need right now though, is people who want to be part of something.

We need people who want to break new ground.

— Matt & Cozza
